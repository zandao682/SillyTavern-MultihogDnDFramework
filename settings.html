                    World Progression
                    BETA
                    

                

                

                    

                        

                            ✨ New Feature: World Progression is currently under active development. It currently lacks important features such as a system to compress the backlog of old reports among other things. Your feedback is highly appreciated!
                        


                        

                            World Progression automatically generates off-screen world simulation reports at a fixed in-world time interval.
                            Reports are written by a standalone LLM pass and stored in the {prefix}_World lorebook.
                            The trigger is deterministic — JavaScript checks the [TIME] block after every State Tracker update;
                            the AI only writes the report, never decides whether to generate one.
                        


                        

                        


                        
                        
                        
e.g. 24 = daily, 12 = twice/day, 8 = 3×/day

                                A medium-intensity review mode. Adjusts the State Tracker prompt with regex tweaks to request complete output from all sections.

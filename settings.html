                    World Progression
                    BETA
                    

                

                

                    

                        

                            ✨ New Feature: World Progression is currently under active development. I am actively expanding and refining its features, and your feedback is highly appreciated!
                        


                        

                            World Progression automatically generates off-screen world simulation reports at a fixed in-world time interval.
                            Reports are written by a standalone LLM pass and stored in the {prefix}_World lorebook.
                            The trigger is deterministic — JavaScript checks the [TIME] block after every State Tracker update;
                            the AI only writes the report, never decides whether to generate one.

                            World Skeleton
                            
                        

                        

                            Provide a description of the setting, themes, atmosphere, and tone of your world directly, or auto-generate it from your recent chat history. The LLM will use this description to generate a set of hidden background entities (factions, locations, NPCs, conflicts) that the World Progression engine will use to simulate off-screen activity. Note: This is in addition to the organically built lore from your narrative. The goal is to simulate activity and events beyond what you have directly seen as a player, expanding the world by incorporating external entities that did not appear in the narrative. This component is optional, but recommended for a richer simulation.
